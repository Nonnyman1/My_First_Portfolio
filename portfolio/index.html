About Me

Dr. Chinonso Charles Ezugwu, a Medical Doctor with a degree from Chukwuemeka Odumegwu Ojukwu University and a Data Analyst
certified by Jupita Academy, currently serves as a Lecturer in the Faculty of Basic Medical Sciences at David Umahi Federal University of Health Sciences, Uburu.
Formerly an Occupational and Environmental Medical Officer at Flour Mills of Nigeria PLC, Dr. Chinonso was involved in research on workplace safety,
significantly improving protocols. His research spans topics such as HPV vaccine acceptance among nursing students and cutaneous manifestations of
Diabetes Mellitus, translating complex data into actionable insights for enhanced patient outcomes. Committed to community service, he volunteers
in rural healthcare and participated in 2020 COVID-19 prevention seminars in Anambra state as part of the resource persons.
Certified in both data analytics and medicine, Dr. Chinonso's skill set merges medical expertise with data analytics proficiency,
driving innovation and excellence in healthcare delivery while advancing medical knowledge.
